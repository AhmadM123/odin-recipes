Hummus

Simple & Delicious Hummus Recipes You Can Make at Home

Hummus is a creamy, flavorful dip that has made its way from Middle Eastern kitchens to tables around the world. It’s a healthy, delicious option for snacking, spreading, or even as a side dish. The best part? It’s incredibly easy to make at home with just a few ingredients. Below, we’ll share three quick hummus recipes: classic, roasted red pepper, and avocado hummus!

Classic Hummus Recipe

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 clove garlic


Instructions

  1. Blend the Ingredients:

    In a food processor, add chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, and salt. Blend until smooth.

  2. Adjust the Texture:

    If the hummus is too thick, add water a tablespoon at a time until you reach your desired consistency.

  3. Serve:

    Transfer to a bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and garnish with paprika or parsley. Serve with pita, crackers, or veggies.
