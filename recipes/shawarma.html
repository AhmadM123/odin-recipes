Master the Art of Homemade Shawarma: Delicious Recipes for Every Taste!

Shawarma is a mouthwatering Middle Eastern dish that's become a global favorite. The tender, spiced meat, wrapped in warm pita or served on a plate with tangy sauces, is truly irresistible. The great news is, you don’t need to visit a restaurant to enjoy this flavorful dish—you can make it at home! In this blog post, we’ll share some delicious, easy-to-follow shawarma recipes for chicken, beef, and even vegetarian versions that will delight everyone at your table.


Ingredients

  • lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 tbsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tbsp ground paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground allspice
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste


Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken:

    a large bowl, combine garlic, cumin, coriander, turmeric, paprika, cinnamon, allspice, yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Add the chicken thighs and coat them thoroughly. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best results.

  2. Cook the Chicken:,

    Preheat your grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat. Grill the chicken for 5-7 minutes on each side until fully cooked.

  3. Assemble the Shawarma:

    Slice the grilled chicken thinly and serve on warm pita bread. Add toppings like tomatoes, cucumbers, lettuce, pickles, and drizzle with tahini or garlic sauce.
