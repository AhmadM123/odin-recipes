Toum! Creamy Middle Eastern Garlic Sauce

Toum is a rich, creamy, and intensely garlicky Middle Eastern sauce that pairs beautifully with grilled meats, roasted vegetables, or even as a spread for sandwiches.

Classic Hummus Recipe

  • 1 cup garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 3 cups neutral oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup cold water


Instructions

  1. Prepare the Garlic:

    Add garlic and salt to a food processor. Pulse until the garlic is finely minced, scraping down the sides as needed.

  2. Start the Emulsion:

    With the processor running, slowly drizzle in 1/2 cup of oil, followed by 1 tsp of lemon juice. Continue alternating between oil and lemon juice, little by little, to avoid breaking the emulsion.

  3. Add Water:

    If the sauce starts to look too thick or the garlic isnâ€™t blending smoothly, add a tablespoon of cold water to help with the consistency.

  4. Finish the Sauce:

    Continue adding the oil and lemon juice until the full amounts are used. The mixture should turn into a creamy, fluffy sauce. Serve: Once the toum is ready, transfer it to a jar or bowl. Serve it with grilled meats, shawarma, kebabs, or as a dip for veggies and bread.

  5. Serve:

    Transfer to a bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and garnish with paprika or parsley. Serve with pita, crackers, or veggies.
